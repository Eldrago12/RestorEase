Common Sleep Disorders

Understanding the symptoms is the first step towards better sleep health

Insomnia

Affects 33% of Indians, particularly high among IT professionals and shift workers. Symptoms include difficulty falling asleep and maintaining sleep.

Sleep Apnea

13.7% of Indians suffer from sleep apnea, with higher rates among middle-aged men. Characterized by breathing interruptions during sleep.

Shift Work Disorder

Common among India's growing BPO and healthcare workforce. Disrupts natural sleep-wake cycles.

Stress-Related Sleep Issues

Nearly 45% of Indians suffer from stress-related sleep issues, particularly in metropolitan areas.
